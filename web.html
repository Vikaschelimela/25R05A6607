movie review

Arjun reddy

Arjun Reddy was a massive hit, considered a blockbuster and cult classic, achieving significant box office success (around ₹50-80 crores globally) with a modest budget, leading to major remakes like Kabir Singh, despite facing controversy for its portrayal of toxic masculinity and substance abuse.

Animal

Ranbir Kapoor's Animal (2023) was a massive blockbuster hit, becoming one of the highest-grossing Indian films ever, earning over ₹900 crore worldwide despite polarizing reviews for its violence and themes. It broke records, becoming the first 'A' rated film to enter the ₹500 crore club in India, proving to be a historic commercial success.
